Boxxing

Boxing is not a sport, it's an art. It combines grueling physical training, intellectual work in strategy development, mental development and analysis of past situations. This sport is for everyone. Both professional athletes and beginners who want to put their body and spirit in order and be self-confident. However, do not forget that excessive sport leads to injuries and serious consequences for your health. Therefore, be balanced in the choice of loads and, if possible, use the services of trainers.
